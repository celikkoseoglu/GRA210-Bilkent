A Note On Time
Workshop.

"Interfaces have the possibility of conveying more than simply utility, they may also transmit a point of view. Interfaces surround us, manifested in compiled code, running on silicon chips, andfronting the computer services we all use, all the time. So, we had better understand at least a little about how they are made."
